For a programming part of this assignment, I used a photo of my face to experiment with image augmentation in python.

Process:

  1. Turn the image to greyscale

    2. I made a copy of the original image, and iterated through it's rows and columns and altered the copy image's color channel by setting it to an average of the RGB channels

  2. Invert the color channel

    3. Then I took the altered greyscale image and iterated through it's color channel and subtracted each value from 255 in order to invert the greyscale colors.
